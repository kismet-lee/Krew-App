What are the benefits of mentorship?

  • Mentees are promoted 5 times more often than those without a mentorship.
  • Mentoring programs boosted minority representation at the management level from 9% to 24%.
  • Promotion and retention rates for mentored minorities and women increase from 15% to 38%, as compared to non-mentored employees
